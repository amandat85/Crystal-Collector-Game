Crystal Collector Game

Rules

  1. You will be given a random number, (target), at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below, each assigned a random number.
  3. By clicking on a crystal, you will add an amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your score to the random number generated at the start of the game.
  5. You lose the game when you go over the generated number.
  6. After each game win or lose, the game will reset and a new number will be generated.
  7. The crystals will be assigned a new value when the game is reset.
